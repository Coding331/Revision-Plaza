Types of Shapes and Their Properties

1. Triangle

Triangle Properties

A triangle is a polygon with three sides. The properties depend on the type:

  1. Equilateral: All sides are equal, and angles are 60°.
  2. Isosceles: Two equal sides and angles.
  3. Scalene: No sides or angles are equal.

2. Square

Square Properties

A square has four equal sides and four 90° angles.

  1. All four sides are equal.
  2. Each angle is 90°.
  3. The diagonals bisect each other at 90°.

3. Rectangle

Rectangle Properties

A rectangle has opposite sides equal and four right angles.

  1. Opposite sides are equal.
  2. All angles are 90°.
  3. Diagonals are equal and bisect each other.

4. Circle

Circle Properties

A circle has all points equidistant from the center. Key properties:

  1. Radius: Distance from center to any point on the circle.
  2. Diameter: Twice the radius, longest distance across the circle.
  3. Circumference: C = 2πr

5. Parallelogram

Parallelogram Properties

A parallelogram has opposite sides parallel and equal in length.

  1. Opposite sides are parallel and equal.
  2. Opposite angles are equal.
  3. The diagonals bisect each other.
