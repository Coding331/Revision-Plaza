Types of Shapes and Their Properties

1. Triangle

Polygons with three sides, classified as equilateral, isosceles, or scalene based on their side lengths and angles.

2. Square

All sides equal, four right angles, and diagonals that bisect at 90째.

3. Rectangle

Opposite sides are equal, all angles are 90째, and diagonals are equal.

4. Circle

All points equidistant from the center; diameter is twice the radius.

5. Parallelogram

Opposite sides are parallel and equal; opposite angles are also equal.
